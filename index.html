Our Story

Born from Passion,
Rooted in Puerto Rico

Empanissah's wasn't just opened — it was built from the heart. Our chef and owner's love for cooking was sparked by Puerto Rican family traditions passed down through parents and grandparents. When life pushed her to start over, she did what she knew best: she cooked.

Today, every dish is a love letter to Latin flavor. Every empanada is folded by hand. Every plate is prepared fresh, every single day. We're not a chain. We're a family — and our food tastes like it.
